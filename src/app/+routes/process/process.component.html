1. Engineering

Primary Goal:

Create an extensible mobile image gallery.

Personal Goal:

Offer an ergonomic implementation.

Extensible & Ergonomic:

gxe-gallery is a Angular component that can render the entire experience from a single html tag. Any data that fits the interface can be Input() into the gallery through the [galleryItems]="data" binding. The underlying services are extensible and the component itself is simple to get up and running.

2. Design

Goal:

Create an Image Gallery and enhanced with motion design

Problem:

Unclear mock up that lacked context and context.

Precedence:

The interaction and motion design were explicitly kept simple, only using Material Design and Google Photos as the design precedence.

Why Material:

Material design's motion is meticulously justified I tried to achieve motion in the UI that was:

Responsive Natural Aware Intentional

Interaction:

Google Photo's Interaction Design is familiar to it's users. And therefore, I leveraged many of the interaction from it.

Open and Close:

Paginate, Tap, & Swipe

Reflections:

In hindsight, I wish I would have explored motion and interaction more. I was nervous to explore because the brief lacked context and time constraint.

If there was more time:

More iteration for the designer An attempt at original Interaction & Motion Design Animated vertical swipe interaction when closing the gallery. Accessibility
